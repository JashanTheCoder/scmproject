What is the purpose of e-learning?

E-learning provides a flexible and accessible way to acquire knowledge and skills using electronic resources.

How long should an eLearning course be?

here isn't a simple and unique response for this question. The variables can be many. But as a best practice, most courses should clock in around the 10-15 minute mark and avoid going beyond 30 minutes.
